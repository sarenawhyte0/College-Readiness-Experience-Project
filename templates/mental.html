Mental Health Disorders

At a new location, it can be difficult for most people to adjust, and this can eventually lead to problems with mental health. As a upcoming freshman, many people may face depression or other mental health disorders while trying to adjust to change.

Learn more about the different disorders college students experience.
Self-Care Tips

It's essential to be taking care of yourself in order to make take a step towards good mental health. It is likely that during college that you'll rarely feel like that you have free time to do things that you like, and so make use of the time that you do have to take care of yourself.

Learn about self-care tips for college students.
Support System

For most people, feeling lonely at a new place is a very common feeling. During moments of stress and feeling overwhelmed, it can be very easy to feel more lonely than usual. It helps to have a trusworthy support system around you to help you during these stressful times.

Learn about benefits of having a strong support system and ways to build your support system.
