Mental Health

The topic of mental health is now being discussed more than ever. We believe in providing student's with more information on how and where to receive help. The transition to college can be a mentally taxing event for many students so never hesitate to reach out if you are struggling! In addition, you can explore why having a positive support system improves the way you tackle mental pressure during college.

Internships

Experience is a college student's golden ticket and the best way to gain industry experience is through internships! Not only do internships provide experience, they open doors for networking, career exploration, and building critical skills. Remember to keep an open mind when choosing what internships to apply to.
